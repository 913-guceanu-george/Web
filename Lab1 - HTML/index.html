Brief smartphone history

In 1992, IBM announced the very first smartphone. It released the Simon Personal Communicator (SPC) for purchase in 1994.
The SPC was the first touchscreen phone. Additionally, it had the ability to send and receive both emails and faxes.

The HTC Dream was the first ever smartphone to ship with the Android operating system in September 2008.

Apple released the first iPhone in 2007. It was a revolutionary device that changed the way we use our phones.
The iPhone was the first smartphone to have a touchscreen, a camera, and a web browser.
